About Our Work

Lukhanyo Educare Centre was established in 1996 by the late Mrs Elsies Khunjuzwa Qhagana, our center stands as a beacon of hope in the heart of Site C, Khayelitsha. Mrs Qhagana's vision was born out of a deep sense of care for the children and families in our impoverished community.

At Lukhanyo Educare Centre, we are more than just an educational institution; we are a family. Our mission is to respond to the unique needs of the children and the community we serve. We understand the challenges faced by families in our area, and we are dedicated to providing a nurturing environment where every child can be developed.

Our Culture
